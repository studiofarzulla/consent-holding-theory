BAYESIAN LEARNING MECHANISM:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
  Update equation:  x*ᵢ(t+1) = [τ₀·x*ᵢ(t) + τ_obs,i·y(t)] / [τ₀ + τ_obs,i]

  Prior precision:  τ₀ = 1.0
  Obs precision:    τ_obs,i = 10 × s*ᵢ  (stakes-weighted attention!)
  Observation:      y(t) = d(t) + ε,  ε ~ N(0, 0.1)

  Key insight:      High-stakes agents learn FASTER
                    (proportional cognitive allocation)
